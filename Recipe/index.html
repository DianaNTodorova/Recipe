Authentic Greek Moussaka

This traditional Greek dish is layered with eggplant, seasoned ground beef, and creamy béchamel sauce. I chose this recipe because it’s one of Greece’s most beloved comfort foods — rich, hearty, and perfect for sharing.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1cm rounds
  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying
  • 100g grated parmesan
  • 2 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp butter, 2 cups milk (for béchamel)
  • 1 egg (for béchamel)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (356°F).
  2. Salt eggplant slices and let sit for 20 minutes. Pat dry and fry in olive oil until golden. Set aside.
  3. Sauté onions and garlic, then add ground beef. Cook until browned. Add tomatoes, cinnamon, oregano, salt & pepper. Simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Make béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, then gradually add milk. Stir until thick, remove from heat, add egg and cheese.
  5. Layer in baking dish: eggplant, meat sauce, béchamel. Repeat if needed. Top with extra cheese.
  6. Bake for 40–45 minutes until golden on top.
  7. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
